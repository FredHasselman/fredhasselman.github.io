What Archetype?

are you lost...
searching...
found...
?

the Lost Archetype...
you might be it
it might be you
you might be like us too

we're never certain of anything, but always strive to be sure
we never feel proficient at anything, yet always work to improve
we're never right about anything, and we'll never stop to figure out why

we get lost sometimes
-perchance by chance-
a subconscious premeditated gone astray
to enhance that feeling of being away
-stalling to advance-
a purposeful dwell
just around the corner from hell
where you
and me
and we
and I
release our homunculi
and
-marvel at their divine dance-
